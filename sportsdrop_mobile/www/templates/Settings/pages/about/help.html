Tutorial

Create your first activity by tapping Post

View a list of your activities which you can Edit and Delete. Just tap the right icon to edit and Tap and Hold an item to delete

Find other people's activities over the internet by tapping Search

You can send a request to join by tapping the toggle. You should notice a Smiley Face icon appear at the bottom of the screen

Tapping the Smiley Face allows you to join the activity

Any activities you have pending will toggle as Orange indicating that your request is waiting for a response

View the locations of your games using the Map

Please do not forget to send us feedback about your experiences using the App. Thank you
